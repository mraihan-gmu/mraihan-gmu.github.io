About Me

I am a CS PhD Candidate at George Mason University. I work under the supervision of Dr. Marcos Zampieri & Dr. Antonios Anastasopoulos. My research focuses on Large Language Models, particularly in code generation, multilingual evaluation, and applications in CS education. My dissertation is titled "Exploring and Adapting Code LLMs for Underrepresented Domains". Expected graduation in Spring 2026.
